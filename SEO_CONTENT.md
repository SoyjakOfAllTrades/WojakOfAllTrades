## FAQ Content

### Q: How is this different from other NFT projects?
A: Most NFT projects use random generation algorithms. Wojak of All Trades lets you actually build your character using limited edition parts. Every part has a finite supply, and once someone mints a specific combination, it becomes rarer.

### Q: What makes parts "limited"?
A: Each accessory, hat, expression, and color combination has a maximum number of times it can be used. Once that limit is reached, no one else can mint that exact combination.

### Q: Why the memestonks/cyberpunk aesthetic?
A: We wanted to tap into the internet culture that birthed Wojak while giving it a modern, tech-forward feel. The memestonks color palette and cyberpunk elements create a unique visual identity.

### Q: How do I know if a part is rare?
A: The platform shows real-time scarcity data for each part. You can see how many are left and how many have been minted.

### Q: Can I trade or sell my Wojak?
A: Yes! Once minted, your Wojak is a standard NFT that can be traded on any compatible marketplace.
